Robo Race

Problem Statement

Design and fabricate an autonomous or manual vehicle capable of navigating efficently through all possible terrains like sand,pebbles,obstacles,bumpers,bridges,marbles,grease,inclines,dips,cliffs,ramps,banks,etc.The vehicle must be ab;e to effectively follow the path .The robots will be tested on speed and how well navigates through the defined path.

Robot Specification

  1. The maximum dimension of the robot can be 30 cm x 30 cm(l x b).
  2. The robot may be wired or wireless.
  3. The length of the wire (for wired robot) should be long enough to cover the whole track and wire should remain slack during complete run.
  4. Max weight must not exceed 6kg.
  5. The power supply can be maximum 24 volts.
  6. Readymade toy car are not allowed.
  7. Battery may be on-board or off-board.

Rules And Regulations

  1. Robots should be as per the given dimensions .

  2. Each team can have maximum 3 members.Students from different institutes can form a team

  3. No entry fee.

    4.

  4. Each member of the team should carry identity card of his institution.

  5. The robot should not damage the track.

  6. The robot must not leave behind any of its parts during the run;else it will result in disqualification.

  7. Unethical behaviour can lead to disqualification.Faculty - coordinators have all the rights to take final decision for any matter during the event

  8. Certificates will be given to all the participants

  9. The track would be constructed on ground and multiple obstacles(which will be revealed at the time of the event) will span the track
