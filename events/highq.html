HighQ

Rules

  1. There are three rounds - General Quiz, spellbee and visual round.

  2. Participants can have a team upto 3 members.

  3. For spellbee, participants have to tap on the desk if they know the spelling.

  4. Use of phone, paper and pen is not allowed.

  5. Second round is written.

  6. Third round is visual round and where the participants are to write their answers on the sheet provided to them.

  7. Participants are scored on the basis of three rounds.

    8. Winners : Three positions are to be selected i.e. First and the Runner up who gets a trophy and a certificate by the judges and a second runner up who gets a certificate.
