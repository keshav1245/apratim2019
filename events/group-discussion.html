Group Discussion


Rules

  1. Participants will be given a topic.

  2. Participants will be given 1-2 minutes of preparation time.

  3. GD MASTER i.e. the judge of the event tells the participants when to begin.

  4. Total time for a GD session is around 6-7 minutes.

  5. GD MASTER notes down the points of the speakers and scores them on the basis of their confidence, manner of speaking, content, convincing ability.

  6. Participants are advised not to speak at the same time since it gets difficult for the judge to listen to the points.

Winners

    Finalists are to be selected after semifinal round, followed by the final round. Two positions are there i.e. 1st and the runner up who gets a trophy and a certificate by the judges
