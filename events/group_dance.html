Group Dance

Rules

  1. Team size: minimum 5 and maximum 30 members limit in a team.

  2. However, at any instant, presence of 4 members on the stage is mandatory.

  3. Time limit: 6-12 minutes (music piece duration) and 5 minutes will be given for stage set up and clearance.

  4. Costumes and props will be included in the judging criteria.

  5. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.

  6. Participants should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.

  7. Reporting time is 2 hours before the event.

  8. The decision of the judges will be final.
