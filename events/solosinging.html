Solo Singing


Rules


Prelims:

  1. Time limit: 2-3 minutes.

  2. Hindi / English / Punjabi songs are allowed.

  3. No recorded music is allowed.

  4. Decision of judges will be final.

  5. Karaoke is not allowed.

  6. Tanpura or its recording can be used.

  7. Registration can be done online or on the spot.


Finals:

  1. Time limit: 4-5 minutes.

  2. Hindi / English / Punjabi songs are allowed.

  3. Karaoke is not allowed.

  4. Decision of judges will be final.

  5. Tanpura or its recording can be used.
